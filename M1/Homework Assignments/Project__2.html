~: Computer Technology and Its Uses :~

The activity of designing and constructing and programming computers is called computer technology. Computer Technology is essential for every organization. Managers use computers for budgeting and forecasting. Computers are also used to perform audits, prepare financial reports and analyze investments. Organizations are moving towards a paperless environment.

Uses Of Computer Technology :

1. Business
2. E-Commerce
3. Industry
4. Medical
5. Airline System
6. Education
7. Weather Forecasting
8. Home

Business:

                 Computers are very important for today's business. Business rely heavily on fast processing of data that can only be done using computers.
Following are the fields in business where computer technology is used :

              1. Marketing
                 2. Stock Exchange
                 3. Banks
                 4. Departmental Stores
                 5. Office Automation

E-Commerce :

                           E-Commerce (also known as Electronic Commerce) is a process of buying and selling products or services over the internet.
Following are the fields in E-Commerce where computer technology is used :

              1. E-Mail
                 2. Video Conferencing
                 3. E-Shopping
                 4. E-Banking

Industry :

                   Computers are used to control manufacturing systems in industries. Computers also help in monitoring temperature and pressure and checking quality, accuracy and measurement needed in the manufacturing process.
Following are the fields in industries where computer technology is used :

              1. Robots
                 2. Simulations
                 3. Computer-Aided Designs (CAD)
                 4. Computer-Aided Manufacturing (CAM)

Medical :

                 Computers are commonly used in some areas of medical health such as laboratories, research, monitoring, pharmacy, etc.
All of these help doctors in :

              1. Patient Monitoring
                 2. Recording Patient History
                 3. Diagnosis

Education :

                 Education is the process of learning. This process can be more simplified and more effective with computers. Many computer based educational software are available for students. These softwares help them to learn, read, count or to speak a foreign language.
Following are the fields of education where computer technology is usually used :

              1. Computer-Aided Learning (CAL)
                 2. Computer-Based Training (CBT)

Airline System :

                             In airline systems, computers are used to control passenger aircraft. Early aircrafts were controlled by mechanical systems. In the modern aircraft, a fly-by-wire system is used. In this system, electronic signals from the cockpit are sent that adjust the flight surfaces. The computer in the pilot's control is linked up among different cities and gives full information about flights and seat reservations.
