Roller Skating as a Hobby


As far as I am concerned, the best and most entertaining hobby is roller-skating. It had made me very curious and I wanted to learn more about it. In the beginning, I wore a helmet, arm and knee guards to make sure I don't injure myself too badly. Before I got the hang of it, I was often out of control. But I practiced and improved quickly. In the early morning, I would go to the park near our house with my father and sister, where our father taught us how to skate. I found it easy and was a quick learner. After learning how to skate, me and my sibling taught three of our cousins and they learnt it too. I think it is a very interesting hobby. It gives pleasure and stirs up emotions. It strengthens muscles and is energizing and exciting. That is why I adore skating so much. I never get tired or bored while skating. On the contrary, I feel really well.
For those starting from scratch, helmets etc. are a must. Roller-skating requires balance and courage.
