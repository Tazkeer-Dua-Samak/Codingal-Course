~: Computer Technology and Its Uses :~

The activity of designing, constructing and programming computers is called computer technology. Computer Technology is essential for every organization. Managers use computers for budgeting and forecasting. Computers are also used to perform audits, prepare financial reports and analyze investments. Organizations are moving towards a paperless environment.

Uses Of Computer Technology :

1. Business
2. E-Commerce
3. Industry
4. Medical
5. Airline System
6. Education
7. Weather Forecasting
8. Home

Business:

                 Computers are very important for today's business. Business rely heavily on fast processing of data that can only be done using computers.
Following are the fields in business where computer technology is used :

              1. Marketing
                 2. Stock Exchange
                 3. Banks
                 4. Departmental Stores
                 5. Office Automation

E-Commerce :

                           E-Commerce (also known as Electronic Commerce) is a process of buying and selling products or services over the internet.
Following are the fields in E-Commerce where computer technology is used :

              1. E-Mail
                 2. Video Conferencing
                 3. E-Shopping
                 4. E-Banking

Industry :

                   Computers are used to control manufacturing systems in industries. Computers also help in monitoring temperature and pressure and checking quality, accuracy and measurement needed in the demolishing manufacturing process.
Following are the fields in industries where computer technology is used :

              1. Robots
                 2. Simulations
                 3. Computer-Aided Designs (CAD)
                 4. Computer-Aided Manufacturing (CAM)

Medical :

                 Computers are commonly used in some areas of medical health such as laboratories, research, monitoring, pharmacy, etc.
All of these help doctors in :

              1. Patient Monitoring
                 2. Recording Patient History
                 3. Diagnosis

Education :

                 Education is the process of learning. This process can be more simplified and more effective with computers. Many computer based educational software are available for students. These softwares help them to learn, read, count or to speak a foreign language.
Following are the fields of education where computer technology is usually used :

              1. Computer-Aided Learning (CAL)
                 2. Computer-Based Training (CBT)

Airline System :

                             In airline systems, computers are used to control passenger aircraft. Early aircraft were controlled by mechanical systems. In the modern aircraft, a fly-by-wire system is used. In this system, electronic signals from the cockpit are sent that adjust the flight surfaces. The computer in the pilot's control is linked up among different cities and gives full information about flights and seat reservations.

Weather Forecasting :

                             Weather Forecasting is a process of predicting the weather. Computers are used to make this process more accurate. Weather forecasting depends on the accurate collection of data. Data is collected from different areas of the world. The computer makes a model based on a lot of factors like air, humidity and temperature. When data is given to this model it generates a forecast of how the weather will change. This forecast cannot be 100% accurate.
Some businesses are very dependent on the weather and they need constantly updated information.
SUPARCO (Space And Upper Atmosphere Research Commission)weather forecasting department offers analysis of live weather data and provides help to make business decisions based on weather forecasting.

Home :

              At home, computers are used to play games, surf the internet, watch movies and listen to music. All these things can be done using a single computer. It is a cheaper and easier solution. People also use computers to keep their daily expense and make home budgets.
The use of microprocessor technology in the manufacturing of electronic home appliances like microwaves, air conditioning, washing machines, sewing machines, etc. has completely changed our way of life.
